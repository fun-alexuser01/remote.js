Remote.js

Remote control your page

Let users use their touchdevice as a remote control for your webapp. Control slideshows, play games, etc. Without installing a app.

Open the RC on your device

The script is just a proof of concept, it needs an API etc to do something with it. You can take a look at the sourcecode at Github if you are interested!
